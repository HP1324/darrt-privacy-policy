Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Darrt (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

Microphone Access (RECORD_AUDIO)

Our app requests access to your device's microphone strictly for the purpose of enabling voice input. This allows you to create tasks and notes by speaking instead of typing.

We do not use this permission for any other purpose such as background listening, voice commands, or audio recording.

Audio captured via the microphone is only used locally for converting speech to text and is not stored or sent to our servers. You have full control over granting and revoking this permission at any time via your device settings.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect or share any personal data or audio recordings. All information created via speech-to-text stays on your device unless explicitly shared by you.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If any significant changes are made, they will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
